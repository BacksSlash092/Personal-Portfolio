Not more, Better.

Greg J. Borrelly | Full-Stack Developer

We often concentrate on more, be it more conversations, connecting more, contributing more and in this pursuit of more, we pay little attention to better.

But what if more is not better?

I imprint this philosophy into every line of code that I write. My specialty lies in treating every project as a unique opportunity to create something new. After all, the last thing you want to see is a generic template representing your or your company.
